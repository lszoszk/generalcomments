                    IV. GENERAL RECOMMENDATIONS ADOPTED BY
                        THE COMMITTEE ON THE ELIMINATION OF
                        DISCRIMINATION AGAINST WOMEN

                                         Fifth session (1986)*

                      General recommendation No. 1: Reporting by States parties

       Initial reports submitted under article 18 of the Convention should cover the situation up to
the date of submission. Thereafter, reports should be submitted at least every four years after the
first report was due and should include obstacles encountered in implementing the Convention
fully and the measures adopted to overcome such obstacles.
