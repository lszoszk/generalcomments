              IV. GENERAL RECOMMENDATIONS ADOPTED BY
                  THE COMMITTEE ON THE ELIMINATION OF
                  DISCRIMINATION AGAINST WOMEN

                                     Sixth session (1987)*

                       General recommendation No. 4: Reservations

     The Committee on the Elimination of Discrimination against Women,

     Having examined reports from States parties at its sessions,

     Expressed concern in relation to the significant number of reservations that appeared to be
incompatible with the object and purpose of the Convention,

      Welcomes the decision of the States parties to consider reservations at its next meeting in
New York in 1988, and to that end suggests that all States parties concerned reconsider such
reservations with a view to withdrawing them.
